§ 02Company Brief

An integrated EPIC contractor,
by Nigerian engineers, to international standard.

Tegrach Nigeria Limited is a Nigerian engineering contractor delivering end-to-end EPIC scopes for industrial operators. Founded 1988 in Warri, Delta State. ISO 9001:2015 certified.

Tegrach Nigeria Limited was founded in 1988 in Warri, Delta State as an engineering equipment and spare-parts supplier to Nigerian operators. Over thirty-seven years, the company expanded into a full EPIC capability — engineering, procurement, installation and commissioning — and today serves customers across the African sub-region.

The discipline mix covers dredging and marine works, civil-electrical-mechanical, facility maintenance, pipe solutions, oil & gas procurement, and overhaul and commissioning. Each capability is held in-house with Nigerian technical leadership and supported by an international procurement network of qualified OEM partners.

The company operates a documented ISO 9001:2015 quality management system across engineering, procurement and field execution. HSE is held as line responsibility — crews are trained, equipped and supervised to international standard.

Operators are served as discrete-scope contractors or as integrated EPIC turnkey programme leads. Project work spans oil & gas, power generation, refining, petrochemical, pharmaceutical and food-processing sectors.
