§ I · Capabilities

Six disciplines.
One integrated contractor.

Each line is staffed by Nigerian technical leadership and supported by international procurement. Tegrach takes single-discipline scopes and full EPIC turnkey programmes across the same six capabilities.

001

Dredging & Marine Works

Reclamation, bathymetric surveys, dry-docking and marine support for waterway access projects across the Niger Delta.

002

Civil, Electrical & Mechanical

Integrated engineering, construction and installation packages for onshore facilities, plant works and offshore support structures.

003

Facility Maintenance

Operations and maintenance crews delivering scheduled and corrective work to keep process facilities running safely and reliably.

004

Pipe Solutions & Services

Pipeline fabrication, tie-ins, corrosion protection and integrity work across hydrocarbon and process transmission systems.

005

Oil & Gas Procurement

Equipment, material and spare-parts sourcing into Africa with international logistics partners and qualified OEM relationships.

006

Overhaul, Installation & Commissioning

Mechanical completion, pre-commissioning and start-up support for refineries, petrochemical units and modular packages.
